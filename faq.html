FAQ

How do I place an order?               ᐯ

To place an order, simply browse our selection of products, select the item(s) you'd like to purchase, and follow the checkout process to complete your order.

Can I make changes to my order?   ᐯ

Unfortunately, once an order has been placed, it cannot be changed. However, if you need to cancel your order or make any other changes, please contact us as soon as possible and we'll do our best to accommodate your request.

How do I track my order?               ᐯ

Once your order has been shipped, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can use this number to track your package through the shipping carrier's website.

How much does shipping cost?       ᐯ

Shipping costs vary depending on your location and the weight of your package. You can view the shipping cost for your order during the checkout process.

Do you ship internationally?           ᐯ

Yes, we ship internationally. However, shipping times and costs may vary depending on your location.

What is your return policy?            ᐯ

We accept returns within 30 days of the delivery date for any items that are unworn and in their original condition.

How do I request a refund?            ᐯ

If you'd like to request a refund for your order, please contact us and we'll be happy to assist you.

What if I receive a faulty item?      ᐯ

If you receive a defective item, please contact us and we'll do our best to make it right.
